Setup above the metro station Rajiv Chowk at Connaught Place, is beautifully developed 41,500 sqmtr park. With innumerable trees and shrubs including round the year flowering plants, it has twinkling lampposts and four water bodies including a cascade. A 350- seat amphitheatre developed for cultural activities adds more value to this park. The central park has proper pathways for people to take a walk in the morning, too
This park is apt for a evening stroll, the path of the park also allows kids with their tricycles. As and then this place is busy with some or the other interesting events for youth making it a hotspot for youngsters off for shopping in CP. Events like musical events ; events related to national awareness etc are quite often held here.

Location : D Block, Connaught Place, New Delhi

Key Attraction : It has fountains and various events are organised here from time to time.

Metro Station: Rajiv Chawk

Closed: Monday

Timings: 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM

Entry Fee: Free

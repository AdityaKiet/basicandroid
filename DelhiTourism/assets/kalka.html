With Nehru Place being the hub of computer accessories and commercial outlets, the Kalkaji District Park is one of the hot spots that doesn't go unnoticed. Additionally, the renowned Kalkaji Temple and Iskcon Temple attract many devotees and tourists to explore their rich historical and religious background, furthering this park’s popularity.
The park is quite convenient, affable and pleasurable with many interesting big shopping malls and outlets, situated within the proximity of this place. It’s interesting to note that the Kalkaji District Park is quite well-known among Delhiites, owing to the ‘Navratras’ celebrated in the Kalkaji Mandir.

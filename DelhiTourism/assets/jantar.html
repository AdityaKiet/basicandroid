Jantar Mantar, with Yantra meaning instruments and Mantra meaning formulae, was built in the year 1724. Situated near Connaught Place, New Delhi Jantar Mantar counts amongst the numerous astronomical observatories erected by Maharaja Jai Singh II of Jaipur.Jai Singh had found the existing astronomical instruments too small to take correct measurement and so he built these larger and more accurate instruments.
The instruments at Jantar Mantar of Delhi display promising brilliance and fascinating for their ingenuity,but accurate observations can no longer be made from here because of the tall buildings around. It basically comprises of the instruments that were used for keeping track of celestial bodies.

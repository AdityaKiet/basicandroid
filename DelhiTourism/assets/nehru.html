Nehru Park of Delhi is situated on the main Panchsheel Road, near Chanakyapuri Diplomatic Enclave. Spread over an area of almost 80 acres, the park boasts of a dazzling collection of blossoming flowers. Then, there are little mounds and rocks inscribed with the sayings of India's first Prime Minister Jawahar Lal Nehru, after whom the park is named.One of the main attractions of the Nehru Park includes the musical concerts, known as the Morning Ragas and art events.
The Music concerts concerts boast of performances by some of the world famous musicians and vocalists of India. Delhi Tourism and Transportation Development Corp (DTTDC) and the education department also conduct free yoga classes at the Nehru Park of Delhi. The park, consisting of swimming pool and a snack bar, also serves as a venue for many stately dinners.

Location : Panchsheel Road, New Delhi

Distance from Airport: 10km

Metro Station: Race Course

Attractions: Music concerts known as morning ragas ,art events and swimming pool.

Open: All days of the week

Timings: 5:30 AM - 7:30 PM

Entry Fee: free

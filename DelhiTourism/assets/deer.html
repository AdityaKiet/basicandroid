Located in South Delhi, this park is a place that can give immense delight to any lover of nature. The Deer park of New delhi is home to many deer, which gives the park the much obvious name ? Deer Park. Different species of deer can be spotted in this amazing park, situated in a city of hustle bustle.
Among other species, the famous Spotted Deer can be found here. The park has a large enclosed area where deer can live, wander about and play with one another, and be in harmony with the nature. At times they also have a friendly fight with each other. This provides much amusement to visitors, especially the kids. Besides the deer, the park also serves as a habitat for rabbits, guinea pigs, majestic peacocks and a wide range of birds.

Location : Safdarjung Enclave, New Delhi-29

Distance from Airport: 11km

Metro Station: Green Park

Open: daily

Timings: 6:00 AM - 6:00PM

Millenium Park google map

Located near Sarai Kale Khan behind Humayun's Tomb. Spread in around 20 acres, this park provides lusty green and peaceful environment to the people.It was constructed in 2004 by the Delhi Development Authority. The park includes a children's park, an amphitheatre and a food court.Millennium Indraprastha Park is one of the longest parks in Delhi. This park was completely made from a landfill site.
There is a food court here right in the middle of this park which has five restaurants which offers light snacks, beverages & quick lunches to fulfill most of the desires. A large pool with fountains surrounded by lights makes your evening with family & friends an absolute delight. There are a number of cultural evenings that are organized from time to time

Kalindi Kunj is a public garden in Delhi, located on the banks of River Yamuna, close to Okhla barrage.[1] Named after Kalindi, another name of river and goddess Yamuna and is situated close to between Delhi and Noida (Uttar Pradesh) border, it also accessible to Faridabad in Haryana, via Mathura Road.The nearest residential colony in Delhi is Shahin Bagh, Jamia Nagar, Jasola & Sarita Vihar. Kalindi Kunj has extensive manicured lawns and fountains that are turned on only in the evenings.
It has now an amusement and waterpark attached with it whose name is "Delhi Rides". Delhi Rides has different wonderful joy rides and waterpark. The 'Delhi Rides' waterpark has a capacity of 3000 people and it can cater 5000 other people at different joy rides at the same time. Delhi Rides was started in 2010 and is open to public from 2012.
